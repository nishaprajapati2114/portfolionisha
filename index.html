My Projects

Railway management system

The Railway Management System is a software solution aimed at optimizing railway operations. It facilitates ticket booking, train scheduling, and passenger information management. Passengers can book tickets online or at counters, while the system ensures efficient train schedules, seat allocation, and real-time updates. It supports secure online payments and generates invoices. Additionally, the system provides comprehensive reports for management decision-making and staff management functionalities. Overall, it enhances the efficiency, reliability, and convenience of railway services, benefiting both passengers and staff.

Restaurant management system

The Restaurant Management System is a software tool designed to streamline restaurant operations. It facilitates order management, table reservations, inventory tracking, and staff scheduling. Customers can place orders digitally, while the system manages table assignments and order fulfillment. It also tracks inventory levels, automates reordering processes, and generates reports for sales analysis. Staff scheduling features optimize workforce management. Overall, the system enhances efficiency, accuracy, and customer satisfaction in restaurant operations, improving the dining experience for patrons and streamlining management tasks for restaurant owners.
